About Our City

Bellasagaram is a model city designed to balance modern infrastructure with sustainability. It aims to create an ideal environment through thoughtful urban planning and advanced technology.

  • Promote **eco-friendly** residential and commercial zones
  • Ensure **optimized traffic** and transportation systems using smart algorithms
  • Maintain **green spaces** and recreational areas for citizens
  • Incorporate **renewable energy** and sustainable resource management
  • Leverage **technology** for efficient city services and governance

    • City Highlights

    Transportation Sector

    Roads, flyovers, bridges, bus stands, and railway systems are designed for smooth connectivity across the city.

    Residential Zone

    Includes houses, societies, apartments, and empty plots planned to support sustainable living for citizens.

    Utility & Sustainable Infrastructure

    Focus on water purification, drainage systems, solar energy plants, and electric power generation for eco-friendly urban living.
