About Bellasagaram

Bellasagaram, meaning “The Sweet Coastal City,” is inspired by Anakapalli’s heritage, jaggery market, fertile lands, and coastal landscape. The city blends modern design with sustainability for a smarter coastal future.

Its planning model integrates ports, fisheries, solar energy, waste recycling, and a green economic structure — creating a self-sustaining, culturally rich, and economically strong city.

Project

Our project focuses on designing a sustainable and smart coastal city using algorithmic thinking. Each zone is planned using concepts from DAA to optimize resources, reduce time, and improve overall efficiency.
