About the Course

DAA helps us understand how big problems can be simplified into clear steps. It connects logic, creativity, and real-life problem solving in a meaningful way.

Algorithms power search engines, traffic systems, healthcare, banking, and every smart feature around us. This course helps us understand these invisible systems.

About Bellasagaram City

Bellasagaram is a smart coastal city inspired by the cultural identity of Anakapalli. It blends ports, fisheries, solar energy, and modern industries to build a sustainable future.
